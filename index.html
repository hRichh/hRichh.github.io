Media, Information and Technology Literacy

The 3 types of Media Literacy
  • Media Literacy

    A set of skills that people can use to analyze and decode the content of media messages that we receive and the things that we send. The media is a powerful tool that empowers people, being able to gain insight on what is happening or being shared can help us prepare and be more aware.

  • Information Literacy

    The ability to identify, discover, evaluate, and use information effectively is referred to as information literacy. Students learn how to evaluate the quality, trustworthiness, and validity of websites and provide proper credit, from effective search strategies to evaluation techniques.

  • Technology Literacy

    An individual with the capacity to function independently with technology is known as technology literate. Being able to utilize technology effectively, properly, and responsibly tools to access, manage, integrate, create, communicate information, and evaluate.

How does Media, Information, and Technology affect society?

  • There are good and bad effects that media, information, and technology make in our society. People should be able to analyze the content that is being shared in the media. There are individuals who get low self-esteem, depression, and other things because of what is being shared in the media. Information that is given to people would get affected by false news that is being shared, which is why being able to decode and analyze the contents is important to receive proper information. Technology causes Adults and the youths alike to fall addicted to using technology which makes us more reliant and fails to give proper care to ourselves. Although there are bad things in these, there comes also good things. Being able to communicate without delay with other people, being able to spread news fast and easy, supporting those who are disabled, etc..

What is Media?
  • The media is a powerful tool to spread information factual and non-factual throughout the world. People who use the media have access to mass communication, news(in television and radios as well as newspapers), magazines and the internet. In research from Nordqvist ”It describes the various ways through which we communicate in society. Because it refers to all means of communication, everything ranging from a telephone call to the evening news on television can be called media.”.
How do you view Media?
  • For me, the way I view media is that it is something that saves and brings people together. Through the use of media we can immediately assess various aspects of information as well as send information to the masses. While the effects of the media is a good thing it also brings harm to other people because of some that spreads false information intentionally and unintentionally. According to new research (Robinson, 2022)“multiple studies have found a strong link between heavy social media and an increased risk for depression, anxiety, loneliness, self-harm, and even suicidal thoughts.”
Do media technologies have the power to transform cultures?
  • I believe one of the reasons why media technology can influence or affect culture is that the constant communication with other people changes the way people think. In more developed countries their culture is being spread towards others through social media(facebook, twitter, instagram, etc.) and this changes the preferences of people in other countries like the usage or what kind of items/products the other uses. “Cultural products and their popularity can influence which media channels people prefer. Conversely, changes in media and ICTs can lead to changes in how we produce culture.”(Poepsel, n.d.).
